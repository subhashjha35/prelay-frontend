Frequently Asked Questions

1. How could an app possibly predict the delays for the next day?

We are using machine learning of course. We trained our ML model with historical data of delays based on the weather conditions at that time. It still needs a lot more data to make it smarter.

2. How accurate is the prediction?

The training process of our ML model is at an early stage. It gives you a good idea of what to expect, due to the weather conditions. We would need at least a cycle’s worth of data, that is one year. Given time, based on some research papers about our ML model, it could reach around 70% of accuracy.

3. What about accidents and events, that cannot be predicted?
That is a very reasonable question. For events like accidents or stolen overhead powerline, etc. our text analysis algorithm is checking MÁV’s official RSS Feed to retrieve the relevant information from the news, such as the reason for the delay, the expected delay and the stations, that can be affected by it, so we could notify you of potential delays in time.
